About

Our main inspiration raised on how to increase the efficiency for establishments to contact with bands in orther to do live music and vice versa.
We know that for small bands is dificult to find places to play, and also for establishments to find bands that fits with the music standards of the establishment.
In Vibely we want to make all those searchs and transactions easyer for both parts, and to foment the proximity music and local bands. Also we want to increase the number of live music events and help small groups to become bigger and more listened. And obilously encorage people to make here own music, becouse what is a life withou music? ;)
